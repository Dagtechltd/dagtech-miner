About this map

Each cyan dot is a real miner currently connected to the DagTech pool, plotted at their connecting IP's approximate geolocation. The thick arcs are our WireGuard backbone — Cape Town relay (af-south-1) and US-West relay (us-west-2) both reach the pool in Ras Al Khaimah. Identifying details (IP, ISP) are not exposed publicly — only the city/country and which relay each miner uses. Map projection: Natural Earth I, with great-circle (geodesic) routing.
